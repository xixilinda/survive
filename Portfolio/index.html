Project

Survive is an educational website that allows users to learn how to deal with dangerous offences and emergencies by providing several series of text-based game. Users can know about different offence rates in different rigions and can choose the game in these regions to play game with the specific stories based on relatively high crime rates in this city.

Besides, users can also view and search different kind of services on the such as police stations and hospital. Most of them are presented with address and telephone numbers. This is to allow users to get familiar with the place they are living in and know what kind of services are available nearby.

Ideation

Producing an crime story game based on crime rates background for the teenagers was our initial idea.The target audience of our website is 18-22 years old college freshmen who have left or are about to go to a new city to study. For such students and their families, safety always comes first. However, the feedback from course coordinator, tutors and other teams showed that we should not just make datasets as background. Besides, we should consider the goal of our application whether teach teenagers to protect themselves from crimes or teach them about types of crime. we realized the existing and potential problems of our ideas, so we made some changes according to that.

According to the suggestion that bring the data into the central part of the game rather than being a background, we changed the main datasets to police stations, hospital and ambulance, and the offence data will be displayed as an introduction and reminder after the game. In this case, the location of police stations, hospitals and ambulances could be used in the game as a key interaction element. Wrong answer explanations in the game will be available as one of the feedback mentioned. Besides, the style of our website was decided as hand-drawn and cartoon style thanks to the advice about sensitivity, the game and the pictures will not be too violent and scary

What's more, We thought the situations of one tyle of crime may be different and complex. Thus, we decided to use map to let users to know their situations in game about where was policestations to help users to make decisions.
